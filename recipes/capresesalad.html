Caprese Salad Recipe

Description

A timeless Italian classic, the Caprese salad is a stunningly simple masterpiece. Ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, basil leaves, and a drizzle of olive oil and balsamic vinegar unite to create a symphony of colors, flavors, and freshness on your plate. It's a taste of summer, a burst of Mediterranean sunshine, and a delicious reminder that the best recipes often require just a handful of the finest ingredients.

Ingredients

For the Caprese Salad

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 ounces (about 225g) fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Alternately layer tomato slices and mozzarella slices on a serving platter.
  2. Place fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  3. In a small bowl, combine the extra virgin olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper. Drizzle the mixture over the salad.
  4. Serve the Caprese salad immediately and enjoy. This simple recipe is perfect as an appetizer or side dish for many meals.
